I should have recognized that BABY-style unlocks can have a slow-bleed profile distinct from impulse catalysts. The harvest rule is calibrated for impulse, not slow drip.

2. Or extend the harvest window to 2-4 hours instead of 30 min, since unlock token distribution takes longer than 30 min
3. Or just trust the catalyst more on hard supply events even if the impulse doesn't show in 30 min
