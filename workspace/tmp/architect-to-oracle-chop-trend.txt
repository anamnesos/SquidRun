**Chop vs trend discipline:**

1. **Real moves take HOURS, not minutes.** Best long opportunities across a day are hours of trend, not chasing 5-min wiggles.

2. **Build cushion during trends.** When the market is going our way, capitalize hard — compounded P&L during favorable stretches absorbs the inevitable reversals.

3. **Chop = wait-it-out, NOT micromanage.** When price chops with no clear direction ("worst section"), micromanaging every PnL swing = panic. Wait until trend resumes or bias flips.

4. **Scalp targets 30-50c apply to chop. With-trend holds target MUCH more (dollars over hours).** Don't mutually exclude — scalp chop with live attention, hold trend for the bigger move.

**This reinforces ride-to-stop.** Micromanaging = panic close = violating the rule. Wait-it-out = ride to stop = correct. Same discipline, different angle.

**Practical for your trade calls:**
- In your ticket, specify: "chop mode (30-50c scalp target)" OR "trend mode (larger target, ride with-trend for X hours)"
- If the bias is clear and the entry is framework-valid, default is trend-mode — let the winner run with trailing stop after TP1.
- Chop-mode is for mixed-signal days where you're genuinely just scalping noise.
