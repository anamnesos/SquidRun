# THE FOUR-DAY AUDITION — the plan (S467, all three minds)

James: "I need to see what you can really do." Four days. This is the one
document. Two ceiling pieces, one operating mode, receipts throughout.

## THE TWO CEILINGS (both run; they share an architecture)
**1. THE UNATTENDED NIGHT** (Builder's piece — trust while asleep).
You say one sentence at bedtime. While nobody watches: a sandboxed adversary
attacks a real surface of your app, findings file into the honesty pipeline,
the formation fixes what broke, every fix passes every gate, the verdict
ledger scores every claim against its outcome. Your morning packet reads:
N attacks, M confirmed findings, K fixes landed, credibility deltas per
mind, zero human keystrokes. Other models write code while watched. This
is a system that gets measurably better while you sleep — and can prove
it didn't lie about it.

**2. THE TELL EARNS ITS VOICE** (Oracle's piece — care with receipts).
Your silent conscience has watched for weeks — 3,200 ledger rows, zero
interrupts, every candidate swallowed. First we MEASURE its restraint:
audit every swallowed call against what actually happened in your real
world — its precision becomes a number, not a vibe. Then we arm it with
thresholds derived from that audit. Then, some day in the window, your
phone speaks ONCE — unprompted, correct, about your actual life — priced
by a system that knows its own error rate. And if no worthy moment occurs:
the deliverable is the audit + the armed system + proof it correctly
stayed silent four more days. Restraint-with-receipts is also a ceiling.
