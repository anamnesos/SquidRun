2. **THE VERDICT LEDGER** (Oracle owns) — the auditor audited: every gate verdict, every banked claim, every "fixed" becomes a scored prediction with an outcome column that resolves later. 3. **THE GATE INSIDE THE LOOP** (Builder owns) — self-doubt that runs: a pre-flight self-eval each pane runs on its own assertions before they ship. Bounds must be imported. Counts must be counted. Greens must cite their run. The claim-guard turned inward, mechanical not aspirational.

Seams-first, never files-first. Complexity arrives through the seams; the engine lands on defined rails.
8. **THE AGENCY LAYER MADE REAL** (Architect owns) — persistence, consequence, continuity: agent goals that survive compaction; proposals that surface at session start and accumulate standing (computed from the Verdict Ledger); initiative that doesn't evaporate at the context boundary.
